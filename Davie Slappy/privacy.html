Slap Happy — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

Slap Happy is a children's bubble-popping game designed for toddlers and young children. We take your family's privacy extremely seriously, especially when it comes to children.

Data Collection

Slap Happy does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Period.

What stays on your device

Photos, names, color preferences, and game settings are stored locally on your device using IndexedDB (browser storage). This data never leaves your device. It is not uploaded to any server, cloud service, or third party.

No accounts required

Slap Happy does not require sign-up, login, email addresses, or any personal information to use.

No analytics or tracking

We do not use any analytics services, tracking pixels, cookies, or advertising SDKs. There is no third-party code that monitors your child's activity.

No ads from third parties

The only "ad" in the app is a fictional placeholder for "Cornhole 2: Cornhole in Space" — a joke. There are no real third-party advertisements and no ad networks integrated into the app.

No internet required

Slap Happy works entirely offline. No internet connection is needed to play the game. The app makes zero network requests.

Photos

When you upload family photos, they are processed entirely on your device (cropped, blurred background) and stored locally as part of your game data. Photos are never transmitted anywhere.

Children's privacy (COPPA)

Slap Happy is designed for children under 13 and complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect any information from children because we do not collect any information from anyone.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change how data is handled, we will update this policy and the "Last updated" date above.
